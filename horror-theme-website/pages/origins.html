Origins

Some of the most terrifying historical events:

  • The Dyatlov Pass Incident (1959) - A group of experienced hikers found dead under mysterious circumstances in Russia.
  • The Black Plague (1347-1351) - A deadly pandemic that wiped out millions in Europe.
  • The Salem Witch Trials (1692) - Mass hysteria leading to the execution of supposed witches.
  • Jack the Ripper (1888) - An unidentified serial killer who terrorized London.
  • The Amityville Horror (1974) - A real-life haunted house story that inspired movies.
